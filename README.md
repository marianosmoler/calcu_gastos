This project was born in an attempt to solve the issue of determining how much money and to who each person has to pay when a meeting was held.

An easy but typical example: 
Five friends meet in a house, one brought beers, other ordered and payed the pizzas and a last one came with a dessert.
So, issues comes when it´s time to pay: 

How much and to who???

Count must be fair for everyone.

This tool gives the answer:
Receveing names and expenses and telling how much and to who everyone has to transfer, minimizing transactions.
